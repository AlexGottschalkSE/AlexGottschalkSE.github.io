About Our Shop

At Alpha Pack Packing, we're not just about products; we're about solutions. Based in Johannesburg, our company is your one-stop destination for all your packaging needs. Whether you're looking for tailored solutions, personalized branding or expert advice on enhancing product presentation, we've got you covered.

We understand the importance of ensuring your items are safe, secure, and presented in the best possible manner. This is why we specialize in offering both standard and custom solutions, paying attention to every detail. Our expertise in protective packaging ensures that goods are transported safely, and our ability to personalize our offerings ensures your brand stands out in the market.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice in the industry. If you have unique packaging requirements, our team of experts is here to discuss and provide the best solutions tailored to your needs.

HANDIWRAP

Our narrow width stretch films (Handi Wrap) are used in place of self-adhesive tape which, because of the adhesive, could tarnish the product being taped.
Handi Wrap sticks to itself without adhesive.

SHRINK FILM

Shrink wrap, also shrink film, is a material made up of polymer plastic film. When heat is applied, it shrinks tightly over whatever it is covering. It is available in a variety of thicknesses, clarities, strengths and shrink ratios.
