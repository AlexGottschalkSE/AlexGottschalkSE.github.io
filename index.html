PGM Group

Our Pioneering business model is based on the accomplishment of South Africa’s
diverse project solutions and resource inefficiencies. PGM Group is a 100% black
owned private company founded by a board of 1 Managing Director.
Together with a team of Project Managers, Cost Managers, Consultants, Planners and
Engineers they remain committed to improve the quality of business ethics. PGM
Group is driven by the dedication and passion for South Africa and its diverse society.
It was borne out of the knowledge that South Africa is an ever growing economy with
intricate and enormous developmental potential.
