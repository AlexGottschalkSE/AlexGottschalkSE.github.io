What We  Do

At PGM Group, we are dedicated to offering quality services that surpass the expectations of our esteemed clients. We focus on building enduring relationships with our clients and customers by delivering exceptional customer service. Our expertise spans a wide range of sectors including Logistics, Mining of various commodities like Silica Quartz, Coal, Chrome, and more. Additionally, we handle Stockpiling, Processing, and trading of these commodities. Our holistic approach involves everything from feasibility studies, system modeling, front-end engineering, and detailed project management.

Our Key Features

Comprehensive Project Management: From conceptual design, feasibility studies to front end engineering, we have you covered.

Safety and Environment: We prioritize evaluations and assessments of safety, health, and environmental criteria throughout all phases.
Pioneering Business Model: Our model is built on addressing South Africa’s diverse project solutions and resource inefficiencies.

Specialized Expertise: We manage programs and projects with specialized planning and contracting expertise, ensuring optimal outcomes. 

Why Choose Us

Proven Track Record: Our success lies in safely delivering some of the world's most groundbreaking projects.

Diverse Market Reach: We address diverse market segments in a new generation of mining with modern technology.

Strategic Partnerships: PGM Group has forged partnerships with specialist mining services companies.
100% Black Owned: We take pride in being a 100% black-owned private company, founded by a dedicated board and driven by a passion for South Africa and its diverse society.
