Our Services 

PGM Group offers an integrated suite of services including Logistics, Mining of key commodities, and Processing & Trading. We combine our industry expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver unparalleled solutions to our clients.

Logistics

At PGM Group, our logistics services ensure smooth and efficient movement of commodities. We leverage advanced technology and our industry expertise to provide reliable logistics solutions tailored to the unique needs of our clients.

Mining of Various Commodities

Our mining division specializes in the extraction of a variety of commodities such as Silica Quartz, Coal, and Chrome. We utilize modern mining technology and best practices to ensure sustainable and efficient extraction processes.

Processing and Trading

Beyond extraction, PGM Group is adept at processing various commodities to meet specific market standards. Furthermore, we are involved in the trade of these commodities, ensuring our clients get the best quality and value.
